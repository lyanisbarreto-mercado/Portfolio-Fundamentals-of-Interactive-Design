Videos

I am currently learning how to use Adobe Premiere Pro, and as a result of my practice, I have created a few films using a variety of techniques.

Video Advertisement

This video was filmed during my time at Valencia College in my freshman year. I learned how to cut video, include external audio like music and voice over, and more. It is a fake advertisement promoting a pencil pouch, making gags about how pencils always seem to disappear from ones hand.

Short Film

This short film was made to practice more skills in Premiere Pro, like color adjustments and audio editing. It is a story of a royal telling their advisor to relax at the end of the day, as they need a break.

Stop Motion

This has been an introduction to Stop Motion for me. Creating a simple and interesting concept, I used a box with the word "Warning" on it, which seemed to act alive. Just for a plush dragon to fall out of the box once it topples over.
