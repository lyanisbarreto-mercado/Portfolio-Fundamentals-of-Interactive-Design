Art Works

This category contains several artworks I have created, and include a variety like drawings, sculptures, builds, photoshop projects and more. Anything that has some sort of meaning or purpose communicated visually is compiled here.

Marc Chagall Sculpture Project - Vexin

This sculpture was done from a group project in my 3D Design Class at UCF. We titled ourselves as “Venix”, and have taken over a month to recreate Marc Chagall’s painting “The Blue Landscape” into a wooden board. This artwork has been presented at Dr. Phillips, and now resides within the Art Building of the UCF Main Campus.

Happy Ending

The drawing was inspired by a story in the Undertale Fandom. A "what if" story that involved loss, grief, and determination. And while many people love the characters from the story, they don't seem to focus on the ending, or know that it even ended. And it was the rare case that stories like these have happy endings. I wanted to remember that happy ending and hope others will appreciate it.

Bone Drawing

This artwork allowed me to practice perspective, structure, form, and straight line inspection. It is a simple step that helps me gain an understanding on how to create something from a still-life, which can build up to figure drawings.
